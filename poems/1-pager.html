Poems Collection Site

This is a portfolio site for the poems I collect.

I chose this font Josefin Sans to bring elegance, a geometric, and modern font but with an added vintage feeling. It works well at larger sizes.
I use different font-sizes for heading, subheading, and content.

The colors are chosen to bring brightness and energy to have the poems uplift our outlook. The main content is in a variant of green called dark jungle green (last color) that provides depth and contrast to other ligher colors used.
